Industrial Strength

Manufacturing Base & Quality Control Framework

1. Advanced Extrusion & Fabrication Plant

Our infrastructure in Foshan encompasses over 107,000 square feet of optimized workshop areas. Utilizing advanced precision double-head cutting saws and automated CNC machining milling platforms, we assure linear and structural corner tolerances stay tightly below strict professional thresholds.

2. Rigorous Pre-Shipment Debugging Stands

To remove structural flaws prior to ocean shipping, MFWCW operates dedicated full scale product debugging lines. All windows and sliding frames undergo physical leveling, weather-seal consistency checks, and manual glide actuation friction audits.

3. ISPM-15 Certified Ocean Transit Heavy Packaging

Glazing materials demand rugged protection. Our logistics bay strictly uses heavy non-fumigation plywood crates built with integrated heavy forklift base runner structures. Multi-layer shock protection films prevent scratching, ensuring clean arrivals directly to jobsites.
