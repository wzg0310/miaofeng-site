Frequently Asked Questions

Can you supply samples before production?

Yes. Sample policy depends on profile complexity — small sample strips or full sample assemblies can be arranged. Contact sales for sample kit pricing.

What are your packaging standards?

We use reinforced ISPM-15 compliant crates with foam and separators. Glass shipments follow certified packing lists to minimize transit damage.

Do you provide installation guides?

Yes. Each project is supplied with dimensional drawings, installation detail sheets and recommended hardware lists.

How to request performance testing?

Specify required standards (ASTM, EN, NFRC) in your RFQ. We can facilitate lab testing or provide existing certificates where applicable.
