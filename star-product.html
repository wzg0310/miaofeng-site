Engineering Specifications & Structural Depth

Our Minimalist Sliding Doors represent the peak of structural aesthetics and modern window wall craftsmanship. Featuring an ultra-slim interlocking profile of only 26mm, these doors maximize the visible glass plane area to provide unobstructed panoramic vistas while maintaining superior structural resistance.

Constructed from heavy-duty 6063-T5 aluminum extrusions, the perimeter frames incorporate reinforced multi-cavity thermal break barriers (PA66 nylon insulation strips). This guarantees exceptional heat insulation performance, drastically improving the overall building enclosure U-Factor and meeting strict localized building code insulation goals and Energy Star metrics across diverse climate zones.
